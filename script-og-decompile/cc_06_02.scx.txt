[center][margin top="242"]October 12th, 2015. Monday.[evaluate expr="291EA4B5281400811400"][%e]
[name]Shinjo[line]“I can’t believe there was something like that in the basement of AH Tokyo General Hospital, though...”[%p]
Shinjo Takeshi frowned as he leaned back into the sofa inside the messy office.[%p]
The Freesia Credit Bureau was located on the second floor of an old multipurpose building in Sakuragaoka.[%p]
At this hour, the twenty-or-so desks in the room were mostly empty, but that didn’t mean that things weren’t busy.[%p]
Research agencies like Freesia did a lot of work outside the office. It was precisely because they were busy that there was no one here.[%p]
Three people were having a serious discussion inside the empty office.[%p]
[name]Momose[line]“Well, I can understand why you’d be skeptical.”[%p]
In front of him was the CEO of Freesia, Momose Katsuko.[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“Why would I waste my time lying to you?”[%p]
Kunosato Mio was sitting further away from him, not even bothering to conceal her displeasure. It was clear she had no intention of being friendly with him.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“W-Well, I’m not saying that I don’t actually believe you. I know there’s a lot of weird rumors about that hospital.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“And now we’ve got living proof, too.”[%p]
Shinjo looked at the other person on the sofa.[%p]
It was a tiny little girl.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“She was really taking care of the patients in the facility?”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“Yeah, that’s right.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Think we can get anything out of her now?”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“You can try, but you might just be disappointed.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Huh?”[%p]
[name]Momose[line]“Well, you see...”[%p]
[name]Yamazoe[line]“I want to go back.”[%p]
[name]Yamazoe[line]“Please... I want to go back. Please let me go back.”[%p]
[name]Momose[line]“That’s all she ever says. That she wants to go back. She won’t answer any of our questions.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“It’s rare to see you having a hard time with anything, Momose-san.”[%p]
Shinjo stood up and dragged an office chair over to the sofa the girl was sitting on.[%p]
He sat down as slowly as he could so as not to scare her, and looked at her at eye level.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Yamazoe Uki-kun, right?”[%p]
He tried to speak as softly as he could, but she still shivered at his voice.[%p]
[name]Yamazoe[line]“...”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“No need to be scared. I’m... I’m a policeman.”[%p]
[name]Yamazoe[line]“A policeman?”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“That’s right. So don’t worry.”[%p]
[name]Yamazoe[line]“If you’re a policeman, then please, help me.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Of course. So first, I want you to tell me who locked you in that dark basement—”[%p]
[name]Yamazoe[line]“These people kidnapped me. Help me. Send me back.”[%p]
Yamazoe Uki looked at Kunosato for just an instant, and then looked back to Shinjo, begging.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“...”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“So I’m a kidnapper, you see. Want to arrest me?”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Don’t make this any more complicated. Hey, Uki-kun, you seem to be misunderstanding things. You were being made to do terrible things in that basement, and now you’ve been rescued.”[%p]
[name]Yamazoe[line]“There was nothing terrible about it... I was just helping everyone. That’s my job.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“...”[%p]
[name]Yamazoe[line]“So I have to go back. Everyone will have trouble without me.”[%p]
That explained why even a veteran like “Momo-chan” was having trouble.[%p]
Shinjo looked at the two and shrugged.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“I don’t know what to do.”[%p]
[name]Momose[line]“Right?”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“From what I’ve heard, things in that basement were pretty bad. I wonder if a normal girl would want to go back there.”[%p]
[name]Momose[line]“I wouldn’t. Well, what we do here might be just as bad.”[%p]
Momose chuckled.[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“I took a look at her scalp, and there’s no traces of a lobotomy or electric shocks.”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“We won’t know for sure unless we do a brain scan, but my guess is that she’s been locked down there for so long that she’s simply stopped questioning it.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Same as an abused child, huh?”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“Yeah.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“I don’t know what to do.”[%p]
Shinjo looked back at the girl, who now seemed as if she was about to cry, and started to scratch his head.[%p]
As of now, the police had no reason to make a move on AH Tokyo General Hospital. None of the evidence he’d gathered had been acquired by legal means.[%p]
He’d hoped that Yamazoe Uki’s testimony would allow her to function as a whistleblower, but that was looking unlikely.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Oh, right, Uki-kun. Do you know this boy?”[%p]
Shinjo took a smartphone out of his pocket. It was one he’d borrowed from the younger officers.[%p]
He picked out Miyashiro Takuru’s photo from the ones he’d taken of the people involved at the Hekiho Academy photoshoot.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“He says that you picked up his smartphone.”[%p]
[name]Yamazoe[line]“I’ve met him several times. But what’s a... s-smart phone?”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Huh? What?”[%p]
[name]Momose[line]“She doesn’t know about anything that’s newer than a few years old.”[%p]
Shinjo thought for a moment, then pointed to his own phone.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Um, this is a smartphone. He dropped something like this, and you picked it up. Do you remember?”[%p]
She stared at the phone in his hands, and then nodded softly.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“I thought so. So what happened to it?”[%p]
[name]Yamazoe[line]“I put it back where it was, right away.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“You put it back? You didn’t take it with you?”[%p]
[name]Yamazoe[line]“I wouldn’t do that. Things I don’t understand are scary.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“But Miyashiro-kun says that he got a call from that phone. Wasn’t it you who did that?”[%p]
[name]Yamazoe[line]“Huh? Smartphones are machines that show pictures like that, right? They can make calls, too?”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“...”[%p]
Momose giggled a little.[%p]
Shinjo glared at her, then turned the phone around and brought up a number from the address book.[%p]
He tapped the number.[%p]
The phone rang for a moment, and then an extremely irritated voice answered.[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“Yes, hello? This is Arimura.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“See? You can make calls with it.”[%p]
Shinjo turned up the volume so that Yamazoe Uki could hear it.[%p]
But she didn’t seem to care.[%p]
[name]Yamazoe[line]“I see.”[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“Hello? What is this about? If you don’t have anything to say, I’m going to hang up. I’m about to go to lunch.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“No, I do have something to talk about. Something important. Can you help me with—”[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“No.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“At least let me finish.”[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“Listen, Shinjo-san, am I supposed to be your girlfriend or something? You’re always calling me. It’s kind of lewd.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“No, no, wait. I didn’t mean—”[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“So you’re a stalker, then, right?”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“I am not! I’m in trouble, and I need your help!”[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“That’s what all the bad boys say. Then you beg me for money, or make me do weird jobs to earn cash for you. You make me sick.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Be serious. This is important. Please.”[%p]
Shinjo finally managed to quiet her down, and then told her about AH Tokyo General Hospital and Yamazoe Uki.[%p]
And then he asked Uki a few more detailed questions, this time so Arimura could hear her responses.[%p]
And as a result...[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Well, Arimura-san?”[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“She’s totally clean. She didn’t tell a single lie.”[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“And she really doesn’t know anything about these incidents. Do you think she’s got something to do with the killer? If so, then you’re wasting your time.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“I see...”[%p]
Shinjo’s shoulders slumped when he heard that. He saw that Kunosato’s did, too.[%p]
She’d probably thought that she could learn a lot of what she wanted to know from Yamazoe Uki.[%p]
Kunosato frowned and looked up at the ceiling.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Thanks, Arimura-san. You were a big help. I’ll take you to lunch or something.”[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“Huh? Are you asking me out on a date? Hell no.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“If I took a minor out on a date, I’d lose my job. Anyway, talk to you later.”[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“What do you mean, ‘later’? Stop calling me! How many times do I have to—”[%p]
He ended the call before Arimura could say anything else, and finally, the room fell quiet.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“...”[%p]
[name]Momose[line]“...”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“...”[%p]
No one spoke for a while.[%p]
The only sound was Yamazoe Uki mumbling, “I want to go home,” as she stared at her feet.[%p]
[name]Momose[line]“Well, we’ll keep her here for a while longer.”[%p]
[name]Momose[line]“But there’s not much we can do beyond give her food and a place to sleep.”[%p]
Momose stood up and rolled her tired neck.[%p]
There was an unhealthy popping sound.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“That’s still a big help.”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“So we’re going to have to start over from scratch with AH Tokyo General Hospital, then.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Not completely. I’ll try to come up with a way to do a real search of that underground facility.”[%p]
[name]Momose[line]“Can you? If you file the appropriate paperwork, they’re just going to pressure you to shut it down again.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“I’m sure.”[%p]
[name]Momose[line]“You’re sure? Then...”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Well, I can come up with an excuse. If it doesn’t work, it doesn’t work, but I think I can do something.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“And if I can get this into the public’s eye, I don’t think they’ll be able to ignore it.”[%p]
[name]Momose[line]“Hmph...”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“What? Did I say something strange?”[%p]
[name]Momose[line]“You’re starting to remind me of them.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Is that something I can be happy about?”[%p]
[name]Momose[line]“I don’t know. If nothing else, you’re probably in for a gruesome death.”[%p]
