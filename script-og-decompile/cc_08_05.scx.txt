[center][margin top="242"]October 27th, 2015. Tuesday.[evaluate expr="291EA4B5281400811400"][%e]
Haida Riko’s condo was cut off from sunlight by the surrounding buildings, making the whole place feel somehow gloomy.[%p]
Kunosato Mio was standing there, her eyes as cold as ever.[%p]
She’d gone through all the wooden shelves and colored boxes on the walls, read every last document and receipt inside, and found nothing.[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“You’re sure there’s nothing in what the police took, right?”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Yeah. I looked at all of it. I’m sure.”[%p]
Several days had passed since the death of the woman they believed to be Minamisawa Senri. The body had long since been taken out for an autopsy.[%p]
A few pieces of evidence, like the table the body had been found on, had been taken by the police, but Shinjo said the clue she was looking for wasn’t among them.[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“...”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line](There’s nothing here. Am I wasting my time?)[%p]
She’d spent two hours searching the room.[%p]
Even she was starting to feel a little concerned, but she shook her head to drive the thoughts from her mind.[%p]
Next, she pulled out the magazines and manga from the shelf, and began to check if anything was hidden between the pages.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Hey.”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“What?”[%p]
Shinjo was leaning up against the wall and watching her, a frown on his face.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“This room was completely locked. There’s no sign of anyone but her ever being here, and the rebar that killed her was something she even brought here herself.”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“What of it?”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Just how did she die? Regardless of whether it was a murder or suicide.”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“I’m not a detective. Don’t expect me to solve your locked room mysteries for you.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“That’s not what I mean. I’m asking for your opinion on how a Gigalomaniac could have died.”[%p]
Shinjo sounded unhappy as he turned to look at the spot where the burnt body had been found.[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“A Gigalomaniac is different from what you’d typically imagine when thinking about someone with psychic powers. Their powers can only be used when there’s someone else around for them to share their delusions with.”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“In other words, if this really was a completely sealed space, which no one else’s eyes could perceive the interior of—no matter ‘whose eyes’ they might be—then Minamisawa Senri would’ve been no different from a normal person. She wouldn’t have been able to use her pyrokinesis.”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“But this place wasn’t completely sealed. Take that window, for instance. If someone was looking in through there, it would’ve been easy for her to share a delusion with them.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“I thought of that too, of course.”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“Then what’s the problem? Tch. Nothing here, either.”[%p]
Kunosato finished checking all the bookshelves and looked around the room, uncertain of what to do next.[%p]
There wasn’t a lot of furniture or appliances. The place was rather plain.[%p]
The closet and desk were the first places she’d checked. But she still hadn’t found what she was looking for.[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line](If Minamisawa Senri really did live here, I was sure I’d find at least some hint of the Committee.)[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line](Or knowing them... Did they get here before I did?)[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line](Damn it. It’s been too many hours since the murder. If I’d been able to look at this place immediately, I might’ve been able to find something first...)[%p]
She looked over her shoulder and glared at Shinjo. It took effort to stop herself from bitching him out.[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line](Hmph, fine. I’ll find them anyway.)[%p]
Kunosato decided to search the whole room once more.[%p]
All the paperwork inside the messy desk used the name Haida Riko. It was nothing but receipts and utility bills.[%p]
But there was no sign of a bill for a phone or internet service.[%p]
She’d had neither, it seemed. There was no sign of her even having a landline phone.[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line](That’s right... There isn’t a phone anywhere.)[%p]
She looked around the room and found the phone jack, but it was covered in dust and showed no signs of ever having been used.[%p]
The only other things she found among the papers were receipts for food and drink from a convenience store.[%p]
Minamisawa Senri had never cooked. Convenience store food was all she ate.[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line](She must’ve spent a lot of money on food, I bet.)[%p]
How had she paid for it all?[%p]
The police were trying their hardest to find out, but there was no sign of a bank book or credit card in the room, and they didn’t know anything about her bank account, either.[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line](The Committee wasn’t paying her? If I could at least find her bank account, I could start trying to trace it...)[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Minamisawa Senri was seen by Miyashiro-kun and his friends between the night of the 23rd and the early morning of the 24th.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“And it was the night of the 24th that she was found dead. Which means she died sometime after her last sighting.”[%p]
Kunosato had been so lost in thought that she hadn’t even realized Shinjo was talking to her.[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“Huh? What’s that?”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“I’m saying that there were twenty hours between the last time Minamisawa Senri was seen, and when her body was found.”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“That’s plenty of time.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“But we have no clue about what happened during those twenty hours, or even what Minamisawa Senri was doing during that time.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“She didn’t even come back to her condo.”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“What?”[%p]
Kunosato’s expression changed for the first time.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“She doesn’t show up on the security footage recorded on the 24th.”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“That’s impossible. Then how did she wind up dead in this room?”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“That’s the big question.”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“The guy who checked the footage must’ve missed it.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“I checked it myself. There was no mistake.”[%p]
Shinjo closed his eyes and rubbed them. He’d caused himself a good bit of eyestrain.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“I’m having someone else check the video now... But I have no idea what this means.”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“...”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“...”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“Fine. I’ll think about it.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Thanks.”[%p]
He covered his face with his left hand, exhausted.[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“I’m going to look at this room a little more and see if I can’t find that out, too. Can you find out for me when Minamisawa Senri began using the alias Haida Riko?”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“The Committee might be involved in that, too.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Yeah, I’m on it.”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“Oh, and when are the police going public with this?”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“It hasn’t been decided. There’s some arguing going on about that.”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“...”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“I think that if it made the news, we might learn more about Minamisawa Senri.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“I know how you feel, but don’t be in such a hurry.”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“...You know? Just what do you know about me?”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“...”[%p]
She glared daggers at him, but Shinjo shrugged and said nothing.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Anyway, I’m off to go get a bite to eat. What about you?”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“I’m not hungry. Go.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“All right, I will.”[%p]
Shinjo left without trying to invite her a second time.[%p]
But as he opened the door, he paused as if he’d remembered something.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“You can look as much as you want, but the building’s manager is suspicious of you. He’s not convinced you’re really with the police. If he calls the local cops, there’ll be trouble, so be careful.”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“I’ll keep that in mind, I guess.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Your guess isn’t good enough. I’m the one they’ll be getting mad at.”[%p]
And then he laughed, but there was no humor in his voice.[%p]
