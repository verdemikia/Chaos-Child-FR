Kurusu was walking alone along an empty street.[%p]
Her breathing was a little heavy. She’d been walking a long time, and at a fast pace.[%p]
She was wearing her school uniform.[%p]
There was a school rule that said whenever you went on campus, you had to wear your uniform. It was funny to realize that she was obeying it even now.[%p]
[name]Kurusu[line]“Hahh... haah... hahh... haah...”[%p]
She’d just found a clue in the Don’t Look video, and asked a certain person to meet her at Hekiho Academy.[%p]
The person had said they couldn’t be there immediately, but that they would call her when they got to the school.[%p]
So there was no need to rush. There was plenty of time until the other person would get there.[%p]
That’s what Kurusu told herself.[%p]
But her body kept driving her forward.[%p]
[name]Kurusu[line](Oh, my phone...)[%p]
She realized she hadn’t looked at it in a long time.[%p]
She must’ve been under more strain than she realized.[%p]
She looked and saw several calls from Takuru and Arimura.[%p]
She thought of stopping and heading back, but then sighed as she realized it was impossible.[%p]
She couldn’t go back to Aoba Dorm now.[%p]
If she saw Takuru’s face, her determination would waver.[%p]
Her determination would waver, and she would change her mind.[%p]
So now, the only thing she could do was go forward.[%p]
...Now that she’d realized the hidden truth.[%p]
[name]Kurusu[line]“I’m sorry. I guess I’m a liar, after all.”[%p]
She apologized to her absent brother and tapped the phone in her pocket. Then she glared at the hill in front of her as she readied herself for what was to come.[%p]
She had to rid herself of any kindness or sentimentality.[%p]
Even if she didn’t intend to have any mercy now, she might find herself having a moment of weakness later.[%p]
At night, the academy was an ominous, dark silhouette amidst the dazzling lights of Shibuya.[%p]
It was as if it knew what was coming.[%p]
Usually there was a guard in the room attached to the front gate, but for some reason, she couldn’t see him.[%p]
[name]Kurusu[line]“...”[%p]
The air felt tense.[%p]
She shook off her uncertainty and went inside.[%p]
She went into the dark Newspaper Club room and turned on the fluorescent lights.[%p]
[name]Kurusu[line]“Oh...”[%p]
Kurusu had decided to wait here for her call, but when she arrived, she found something unexpected.[%p]
It was the map that they’d put up to analyze the case, which had been taken down a while before.[%p]
It was now back up on the wall.[%p]
And there were several new pictures and pieces of information that she hadn’t seen before.[%p]
That was probably Takuru’s doing.[%p]
He’d been continuing to hunt the killer in secret, too.[%p]
[name]Kurusu[line](Takuru... You’re a liar, too.)[%p]
[name]Kurusu[line]“...”[%p]
She felt herself letting her guard down as she thought of Takuru, and quickly shook her head.[%p]
She brought a chair close to the wall, and sat down and stared at the map.[%p]
Takuru had said something, once.[%p]
[name]Miyashiro[line]“But now... it feels like the case is pursuing us.”[%p]

But if the killer was just after Takuru’s life, there were more direct ways to go about it.[%p]
But instead, they enacted the Return of the New Gen Madness killings... and claimed Yui’s life in the process.[%p]
It would’ve been easy for them to take Takuru’s life as well.[%p]

[name]Kurusu[line](They aren’t after Takuru’s life. They’re after something else involving him...)[%p]

All of Senri’s possessions were lying beneath the memorial.[%p]
...Kurusu had put them there herself.[%p]
All of Senri’s family had died in the earthquake. She had no surviving relatives.[%p]
So after the memorial had gone up, Kurusu had put them there.[%p]
Even Kawahara, the other boy who’d often played with them, didn’t know that.[%p]
It was a secret about Senri that only Kurusu knew.[%p]
[name]Kurusu[line]“...”[%p]
But Haida Riko also knew that secret.[%p]
No, she’d been given it.[%18]

[name]Kurusu[line](Mind reading...)[%p]

And that was how they were able to find Senri’s ID card.[%18]
[name]Kurusu[line](In other words, it’s someone who I’ve made contact with.)[%p]
And now that she’d found what was hidden in the Don’t Look video... she had no choice but to fight them.[%p]
She’d probably made contact with them more than once or twice.[%p]
Because they were one of her closest friends...[%p]
[name]Kurusu[line]“I wonder how long I’ve known you for...”[%p]
She suddenly remembered their smile.[%p]
Their whisper echoed off the walls, with no one to hear it.[%p]
The countless after school hours they’d spent together were some of the most important times in Kurusu’s life.[%p]
Meaningless conversations, shops they’d browsed together... but she couldn’t think of any special memories.[%p]
And that was very painful for her.[%p]
Because to Kurusu, the two of them were such natural parts of each other’s lives.[%p]
(But I have to ask. I have to find out.)[%p]
Even if the answer meant that things would never be the same.[%p]
