Smiles.[%p]
There were always smiles in Arimura’s family back then.[%p]
[name]Shingo[line]“Look, Hina. You’ve got some cream stuck on your lips.”[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“Oh no! Get it off, Big Bro!”[%p]
[name]Shingo[line]“Fine, fine. There, I got it.”[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“Thanks, Big Bro!”[%p]
[name]Arimura’s Father[line]“You really do love your big brother, don’t you?”[%p]
[name]Arimura’s Mother[line]“That’s right. But it’s always such a big help to have Shingo-chan take care of Hina-chan.”[%p]
[name]Arimura’s Father[line]“Keep it up, Shingo.”[%p]
Their mother and father were both busy with work. Sometimes they’d have to sleep at the company, and they didn’t spend a lot of time at home.[%p]
But when they were with the family, they always smiled.[%p]
And Hinae had a big brother, too.[%p]
Just having her brother by her side was enough for her not to feel lonely. She could, and did, believe that she was part of a happy family.[%p]
For young Hinae, that was her whole family. That was what was true for her.[%p]
One day, Hinae was bullied a little at school.[%p]
It started with something small: someone said that they lost the basketball game in gym class because of her.[%p]
Hinae was a tiny little girl, and the big ball scared her. When they passed it to her, she’d ducked away.[%p]
When her brother Shingo saw she was down, he immediately asked her...[%p]
[name]Shingo[line]“What’s wrong? Is someone bullying you?”[%p]
Hinae shook her head.[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“No. I’m not... being bullied.”[%p]
She was embarrassed.[%p]
She didn’t want her family to know that someone was mad at her for something.[%p]
So that was why, in that moment, she lied.[%p]
But her brother had known immediately that she wasn’t telling the truth.[%p]
[name]Shingo[line]“Hinae, lying is wrong. Whatever you do, don’t tell lies. I don’t want you turning into that kind of coward.”[%p]
It was the first time she’d ever seen her brother look so strict.[%p]
Her brother hated lies more than anybody.[%p]
She’d been so impressed by her beloved brother that she’d sworn to herself that she’d never tell a lie.[%p]
She couldn’t grow up to be a coward.[%p]
She wasn’t going to tell any lies.[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“Actually, I...”[%p]
Hinae told her brother everything.[%p]
That she’d been bullied at school. That it was over something that had happened at gym class.[%p]
[name]Shingo[line]“Want to practice with me, then?”[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“Can I?”[%p]
[name]Shingo[line]“Sure. If you get good, nobody will be mad anymore.”[%p]
With her brother’s help, Hinae got over her fear.[%p]
And the bullying stopped, and her friends came back.[%p]
She came to love her brother even more.[%p]
But one day...[%p]
Hinae realized that all her happiness was nothing but an illusion.[%p]
It started when she witnessed her parents cheating—[%p]
