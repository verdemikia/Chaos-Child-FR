[center][margin top="242"]At the same time.[evaluate expr="291EA4B5281400811400"][%e]
[center][margin top="242"]Dogenzaka, Shibuya.[%e]
The sky had been overcast all day, and now it had finally begun to rain.[%p]
Some of the news media that had been swarming around the love hotel decided it was a good time to go home. Others just took refuge in their vans, frowning.[%p]
Meanwhile...[%p]
Shinjo’s mind raced as quickly as the men trying to flee the storm.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line](...What’s going on here?)[%p]
It had all started right after midnight.[%p]
Arimura Hinae, the Hekiho girl, had refused to give even her name until he’d relayed Momose’s message to her. But as soon as she heard it, her attitude changed completely.[%p]
His only choice now was to continue to do everything Momose’s kid told him to do.[%p]
The first thing she’d said was that there was almost no chance Arimura was the killer, and that it was safe to let her go.[%p]
The second thing she’d said was that it would be smarter to let her go and see what she did.[%p]
The third thing she’d said was that even if he did get some answers out of her, he wouldn’t be able to make sense of them.[%p]
Shinjo had used every bit of his authority to get her released.[%p]
He’d been forced to suck up to some people he really didn’t like, and in the end, she would be monitored and questioned by the police daily.[%p]
Since they hadn’t known if she had anything to do with Kakita’s death, they’d used her assault on the police officer to bring her in.[%p]
Either way, last evening marked 48 hours since her arrest. They had to decide whether to let her go or seek a warrant for detention.[%p]
They hadn’t found any physical evidence connecting her to Kakita’s murder, and Arimura herself said,[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“I don’t remember anything.”[%p]
That was all she’d said, in fact.[%p]
Provisional release at this point felt like a bit of a stretch of his authority, but it was a sensible decision.[%p]
If anything here wasn’t sensible, it was the fact that he was doing everything Momose’s acquaintance asked of him.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“...”[%p]
He didn’t have a plan to follow. This was the first time that had happened, he thought.[%p]
He only managed to be successful at his age around what Momose called the “hardheads” in Section 1 because he knew how to be flexible.[%p]
The reason that many police officers could be stubborn and hard-headed was because most of the time, that was how you solved a case.[%p]
Shinjo understood that the rules they clung to so fiercely, both written and unwritten, were the product of valuable experience.[%p]
But he also understood that the unorthodox methods his senior detective friend had taught him were a weapon in their own right.[%p]
Even if the higher-ups didn’t like him using them, they’d brought him success.[%p]
One good example was the time the profilers had told them that the person behind a string of assaults was a man in his twenties who never left his house.[%p]
He’d found out that the culprit was actually a runaway girl in her late teens who “lived” in a net café.[%p]
He’d matched the internet logs that Section 1 had gathered with the data held by the [color index="280AA02D1400"]CSD[color index="800000"], a group that was on notoriously bad terms with Section 1.[%p]
In the end, he’d cracked the case by logging onto an online game and tracking down several of its players, including the girl behind the attacks.[%p]
Shinjo was, if nothing else, confident that he was capable of not letting preconceptions keep him from making the best choice.[%p]
But this time, not only did he not know what was the best choice, he didn’t even know if the choices he was making were good or bad.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line](...This is ending up just like Momose-san said it would.)[%p]
If he couldn’t change things fast, he might end up being taken off the case.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Hmm...”[%p]
He looked at his watch and realized something.[%p]
The man he was waiting for wasn’t here.[%p]
It had cost him quite a bit to get permission to show him the crime scene, and he was over 30 minutes late.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line](Momose-san said that there was no way I could miss him...)[%p]
He didn’t know what he looked like, but there was no way the officer standing guard outside could miss him—[%p]
[name]Policeman[line]“H-Hey, wait! You!”[%p]
He heard a loud voice from the hallway.[%p]
Shinjo decided that before he let him into the scene, he’d give him a lecture about his tardiness.[%p]
He stopped, surprised.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line](...A girl?)[%p]
He didn’t expect to see what he saw.[%p]
He’d convinced himself that anyone weird enough to want to see a crime scene had to be a guy.[%p]
But she looked like she’d be very pretty with make-up on, even if she wore none now. A frown was plastered on her face and her long hair was halfheartedly tied behind her back.[%p]
She was also wearing a white lab coat, but under it was...[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line](A uniform? And isn’t that...)[%p]
That uniform was for Hekiho Academy, the same school Arimura and the two kids who’d snuck into the crime scene attended.[%p]
Shinjo motioned to the officer to let her pass, and remembered the last name he’d been given by Momose.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Are you Kunosato-san?”[%p]
[name]Woman[line]“Yeah, I leave it to you all. So you don’t need my confirmation.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Huh...?”[%p]
[name]Woman[line]“There’s no time to waste, as you know. Yeah... yeah.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“...”[%p]
[name]Woman[line]“Well, I gotta nurse this dumb cop... Okay, later, Kurisu.”[%p]
[name]Woman[line]“...”[%p]
She then hung up the phone and glared at Shinjo.[%p]
She showed no sign of apologizing for her lateness, or gawking at the scene of a murder.[%p]
“...This is her. It has to be.”[%p]
When he saw how arrogantly she was behaving, he knew immediately that this had to be the Kunosato person that Momose had told him about.[%p]
He felt a little overwhelmed by her, but tried to make his voice as strong as he could.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“...Are you Kunosato-san? Knowing Momose-san, I figured I’d be ready for anything, but I can’t let a young girl into a crime scene—”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“Don’t act normal when you’re surrounded by things that aren’t normal. Didn’t she tell you that would get in your way?”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Hey!”[%p]
She ignored Shinjo’s words and walked right past him.[%p]
She stepped right on top of the number plates that Forensics had put on the ground to identify the locations of evidence.[%p]
Then she looked at the blinds placed on the window to stop the media from looking inside and stopped.[%p]
Shinjo felt himself getting annoyed by her attitude, particularly what she’d just said about him in English.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Dumb cop or not, you’re going to do what I tell you. Right now—”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“People think that every corpse is dead.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“...Huh?”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“You’ve got ears that can understand English, and you don’t watch [color index="280AA02D1400"]Vincher[color index="800000"]?”[%p]
She put her hand up to the blind that she’d been looking at.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Wh-What are you doing?!”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“I’m asking if you really think this room is the only crime scene in this case.”[%p]
She kept her eyes on the exposed window...[%p]
...as she tossed him a PokeCom she’d fished out of her pocket.[%p]
Shinjo grabbed it without thinking and looked down at it.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“...!”[%p]
He was stunned.[%p]
It was footage of the room he was in right now.[%p]
That was probably Kakita, dead, and Arimura Hinae.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“What is this?”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“A video that was just uploaded to the web.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“...”[%p]
Her answer made Shinjo feel pissed off at everything, including himself.[%p]
Why didn’t he notice?[%p]
Why did he have to be shown this by a girl who he’d just met?[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line](... From outside the window?)[%p]
He grabbed onto the window frame, looking for the spot where the video had been recorded from.[%p]
But all he saw was the external wall of the building, slick with rain.[%p]
There was only some kind of strange, creepy sticker there.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Hey!”[%p]
Shinjo yelled out into the hallway.[%p]
The officer outside heard the tone of his voice and rushed inside. Shinjo pointed at the window and said,[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Seal off the building next door right now and get me forensics! And then track down where this video—”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“I don’t know about the video, but you’re probably not going to find anything next door. There’s no sign of a camera having been placed on that wall.”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Huh?”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“I copied the data at the security entrance and Freesia’s team is going over it—but we’re probably not going to find anything.”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“We’d better not.”[%p]
She must’ve been enjoying this, because Kunosato’s frown twisted into a mischievous bully’s expression.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“...”[%p]
Again, he didn’t understand. He didn’t have time for this.[%p]
He ignored her and ordered his men to seal the building, call forensics, and track down where that video was filmed.[%p]
With that finished, he turned back to her.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Is that why you were late?”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“Focus on securing the scene. @channel picked up on this video right after it was posted, and it was uploaded only 40 minutes ago.”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“More than five thousand people have already seen it. You don’t think any of them are with those media people down below?”[%p]
[name]Policeman[line]“Wait! You can’t—”[%p]
[name]Watabe[line]“This is Watabe with Niconiya News! I’d like your comment on—”[%p]
[name]Deciphering Reporter[line]“I’m with Kaiuri TV! I’d like to ask you about—”[%p]
[name]Policeman[line]“Get out! No pictures!”[%p]
He could hear yelling coming from the hallway.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Damn it! That net reporter again?”[%p]
Shinjo ran out into the hallway, infuriated.[%p]
[name]Watabe[line]“Are you with Section 1? Did you see the video—”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Please don’t come in without permission! We have no comment!”[%p]
[name]Policeman[line]“N-No pushing!”[%p]
[name]Watabe[line]“Did you see it? Or not!”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“No comment!”[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“...”[%p]
Kunosato was still looking outside the window as she listened to the commotion in the hallway.[%p]
She stuck her hands in her pockets, and stared at the sticker on the opposite wall the way a person on top of a tall building stares down at the tiny people on the street below.[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“Hey, how does it feel?”[%p]
The sticker pictured two ugly, overlapping faces.[%p]
The rain made it look like they were crying.[%p]
[name]Kunosato[line]“If there was any witness—it was you, right?”[%p]
Kunosato smirked like a fighter facing down a worthy opponent.[%p]
