[center][margin top="242"]November 6th, 2009. Friday.[evaluate expr="291EA4B5281400811400"][%e]
Arimura Hinae was in downtown Shibuya when it happened.[%p]
Her brother’s birthday was coming up in a week.[%p]
What should she get him for a present?[%p]
With those thoughts running through her head, Hinae had taken the allowance she’d saved up over the last several months and went into the city alone.[%p]
Every year, she and her brother would both celebrate each other’s birthdays, just the two of them.[%p]
Hinae would give her brother a present, and he’d say thank you and give her something back, even though it wasn’t her birthday.[%p]
It always made her so happy.[%p]
What should she get him this year?[%p]
She wanted to get him something he would keep forever.[%p]
Something he could wear would be best.[%p]
Clothes were outside her budget, and gloves or a muffler would only be useful in winter.[%p]
What about a wallet?[%p]
Just when she’d decided to go into a slightly grown-up department store...[%p]
The huge earthquake struck Shibuya.[%p]
It was the disaster they called the Shibuya Earthquake.[%p]
Hinae didn’t really remember the moment it happened.[%p]
One second, there was a bright light that flooded her field of vision. And the next second, everything had changed.[%p]
The walls of the buildings had collapsed.[%p]
The asphalt had split open.[%p]
And here and there she could hear people moaning in pain.[%p]
It was like something out of a dream—or a nightmare.[%p]
She didn’t know what had happened.[%p]
She quickly dialed her brother, but not only did he not pick up, she couldn’t even hear it ring.[%p]
Since she had nothing else to do, she decided to head for home.[%p]
Only when she stood up did she realize that she’d been hurt all over.[%p]
Her knees were cut open. She’d hit her back. She’d scraped her cheeks against the ground.[%p]
And all of Shibuya was in a panic.[%p]
The buildings looked like they could fall down at any moment.[%p]
A car had smashed into a store and was giving off black smoke.[%p]
Everyone in Shibuya was running to the station in search of a way out.[%p]
It was hard for tiny little Hina to go against the crowd, and she was almost pushed down many times as she headed for home.[%p]
She didn’t honestly remember how long it took her to get back to the condo where she lived.[%p]
She just remembered that she’d felt so tired.[%p]
But when she saw her condo building, all that exhaustion vanished.[%p]
The building hadn’t collapsed, but it was tilting and looked like it could fall over at any moment.[%p]
The people who’d escaped from inside were standing a good ways away and looking up at it.[%p]
Some people tried to go inside to get their valuables. Others tried to stop them.[%p]
She saw her parents there.[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“Dad! Mom!”[%p]
[name]Arimura’s Mother[line]“Hina-chan!”[%p]
[name]Arimura’s Father[line]“Hinae! You’re safe!”[%p]
Maybe they’d happened to come home early. Or maybe they’d raced over here after the earthquake.[%p]
Either way, they were injured, but all right.[%p]
But there was someone missing.[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“Hey... where’s Big Bro?”[%p]
She looked around, but didn’t see him anywhere.[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“Where is he? Is he still at school? Or with a friend?!”[%p]
Her parents didn’t answer.[%p]
But she could tell where her brother was from where her mom was looking.[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“No way...”[%p]
The condo building was tilted and could fall over at any time.[%p]
Who knew what was going on inside?[%p]
[name]Arimura’s Father[line]“Hinae...”[%p]
Her father slowly shook his head.[%p]
[name]Arimura’s Mother[line]“...Your brother is already...”[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“No... You’re lying... You’re lying!”[%p]
[name]Arimura’s Mother[line]“Hinae...? Hinae! No, Hinae!”[%p]
She dodged past her father’s outstretched hands and ran into the building.[%p]
Obviously, the elevator didn’t work.[%p]
She ran up the stairs, which were only barely still functional, and headed for her condo.[%p]
All the exhaustion she’d just felt was totally gone.[%p]
All she was thinking about was her brother.[%p]
All she wished for was that her brother would be safe.[%p]
Their condo was a disaster.[%p]
There was no sign left of the life they’d lived.[%p]
But that didn’t matter to her.[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“Big Bro! Big Bro, where are you?!”[%p]
She screamed.[%p]
Again and again.[%p]
[name]Shingo[line]“Gh... Ngh...”[%p]
She heard a small moan from under the rubble.[%p]
She ran over and saw her brother.[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“BIG BRO!”[%p]
He was alive.[%p]
[name]Shingo[line]“Hina... I’m so glad... You’re safe...”[%p]
Her brother was still alive.[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“What are you doing, Big Bro?! We have to get out of here—”[%p]
She then quickly swallowed her words.[%p]
Her brother’s body was half-buried under rubble, and he couldn’t move.[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“Just wait! I’ll move it!”[%p]
She tried to lift it off, but she was far too weak to even budge it.[%p]
[name]Shingo[line]“It’s impossible, Hina... Just get out of here... this place is going to...”[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“What are you talking about?! I can’t do that!”[%p]
She tried so hard to move the rubble that her nails broke and blood began to drip from her fingers.[%p]
But she still kept trying.[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“You’re... You’re all I have! You’re my only brother!”[%p]
Her mother and father had already betrayed her.[%p]
The one bond she had left, the one truth she had left, was her brother.[%p]
It was the one thing that kept her going.[%p]
However...[%p]
[name]Shingo[line]“I’m sorry, Hina... but you’re wrong...”[%p]
She didn’t know why her brother was suddenly apologizing.[%p]
[name]Shingo[line]“We’re... not exactly brother and sister... You and I aren’t full siblings...”[%p]
It was a sudden confession.[%p]
What was he talking about?[%p]
They weren’t “full siblings”?[%p]
What did he mean—[%p]
He kept talking, even though Hinae didn’t understand what he was saying.[%p]
[name]Shingo[line]“Hina... you aren’t Mom and Dad’s daughter... Your father is another man...”[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“You’re... lying...”[%p]
Suddenly, the whole world went black.[%p]
[name]Shingo[line]“I always thought that I shouldn’t tell you... That I should hide it until you grew up... But...”[%p]
[name]Shingo[line]“But I’m... so...”[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“Big Bro!”[%p]
[name]Shingo[line]“I’m sorry, Hina... for never telling you.”[%p]

[name]Arimura[line]“No... no more...”[%p]

She saw something in the corner of her eye.[%p]

It was like some kind of enchanting creature.[%p]
But cold, like something inorganic.[%p]
It looked like something similar to a sword...[%p]
[name]Shingo[line]“I’m sorry, Hina... But... it’s the truth.”[%p]
Suddenly, she knew that he really was telling the truth.[%p]
It wasn’t just intuition.[%p]
She knew.[%p]
Somehow, she knew.[%p]
For some reason, she knew that his words were true.[%p]
[name]Shingo[line]“...!”[%p]
As she stood there in shock, she was suddenly knocked backward and almost fell to her feet.[%p]
[name]Shingo[line]“It’s too dangerous! Go, Hina!”[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“Big Bro...”[%p]
[name]Shingo[line]“Please! You... You have to survive!”[%p]
There was truth in his cry.[%p]
She staggered back, just as the roof in front of her fell in.[%p]
The dust rose up so thick that she couldn’t see her brother anymore.[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“Big Bro...!”[%p]
[name]Shingo[line]“RUN! RUN, HINA!”[%p]
...Those were the last words she ever heard him say.[%p]
