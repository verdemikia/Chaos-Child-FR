[center][margin top="242"]October 26th, 2015. Monday.[evaluate expr="291EA4B5281400811400"][%e]
Just like she’d promised Takuru, Arimura Hinae stuck to the busy roads on her way back.[%p]
She remembered Takuru’s face when they’d said goodbye.[%p]
He was probably scared himself, but he was holding it in and pretending to be brave.[%p]
Anybody could see that he was just putting on an act.[%p]
He was easy to figure out...[%p]
And that made Hinae feel at peace.[%p]
Everyone was hiding their true feelings, their truth, deep within their heart.[%p]
There was nothing true in this world at all. That’s what she’d thought that day.[%p]
[center][margin top="242"]Never tell a lie.[evaluate expr="291EA4B5281400811400"][%e]
Her brother had been the one who taught her that, but even he didn’t tell her what was really important.[%p]
He didn’t tell her the truth.[%p]
So where was the truth?[%p]
That was the question she’d been grappling with for the last six years.[%p]
[name]Arimura[line](Big Bro...)[%p]
A friend she’d made just recently had asked if she wanted to see her brother.[%p]
Hinae almost never talked about herself, but she’d been talking about her family.[%p]
And then her friend had asked her.[%p]
She didn’t know why.[%p]
But the question had surprised her, and she’d nodded yes.[%p]
She didn’t hate her brother for lying to her.[%p]
And even if she did, he was gone.[%p]
Because on that day, she’d left him to die.[%p]
So instead, she felt guilty toward him.[%p]
If she ever had the chance to meet him again, she wished to apologize to him.[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“...”[%p]
The crosswalk sign turned red, and Hinae stopped walking.[%p]
Even at night, Shibuya was crawling with people.[%p]
With this many people around, she figured it wouldn’t be easy for someone to attack her.[%p]
As soon as the traffic light turned green, the cars started to move.[%p]
And then—[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“Ah...!”[%p]
Someone pushed her hard, and she staggered out onto the street.[%p]
The headlights were bearing down on her before she even had time to think.[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“...!”[%p]
When she finally opened her eyes, the car bumper was just a few centimeters from her face.[%p]
[name]Man[line]“Hey, are you okay?”[%p]
[name]Man[line]“What are you doing? That’s dangerous!”[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“Ah...”[%p]
The people waiting for the light started to make a fuss.[%p]
The sound of a dozen car horns echoed off the sides of the buildings.[%p]
But Hinae didn’t hear any of that.[%p]
She was only looking at one thing.[%p]
It was the place she’d been standing a moment ago.[%p]
Just before she’d almost been hit, she’d looked up, and saw him.[%p]
[name]Arimura[line]“Big... Bro...”[%p]
...It was her dead brother, Shingo.[%p]
