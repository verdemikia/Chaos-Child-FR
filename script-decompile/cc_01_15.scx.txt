[center][margin top="242"]Time passed. It was now almost midnight.[evaluate expr="291EA4B5281400811400"][%e]
Under a bridge in Shibuya...[%p]
She walked with her head down, so that others couldn’t see her strange face.[%p]
She was dragging her leg, so her body tilted a little.[%p]
She didn’t have an umbrella, so the rain that had fallen that evening made her long bangs cling to her forehead. Water dripped off of them.[%p]
The eerie eyes that poked out from between her locks of hair looked like those of an animal hunting its prey.[%p]
No one was moving but her.[%p]
Normally, there would be people here at this hour.[%p]
But the town left her alone, as if it had sensed something.[%p]
She disappeared into an alley without anyone else noticing her.[%p]
It was several minutes later when they appeared there.[%p]
[name]Young Girl[line]“...”[%p]
A girl less than ten years old, and an old woman well over seventy.[%p]
The old woman might have been blind, because she carried a stick and had her hand on the girl’s shoulder.[%p]
And the little girl was carrying something far too big for her tiny frame.[%p]
Neither of them were carrying umbrellas, and both were soaking wet.[%p]
They walked at a slow pace.[%p]
Perhaps they knew the path well, or perhaps they simply didn’t care, because they paid no attention to anything around them.[%p]
And then, for the first time, the little girl saw something and stopped.[%p]
The old woman stopped as well, and the only sound in the alley was the sound of the rain.[%p]
[name]Young Girl[line]“...”[%p]
The girl stood there for a full minute.[%p]
Eventually, she turned her head and led the older woman away.[%p]
All that was left there was the rain...[%p]
...and the things on the wall.[%p]
