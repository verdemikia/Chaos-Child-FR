[name]Shinjo[line](I see.)[%p]
He’d just come to check on her a moment ago, but they’d been probably doing this since yesterday.[%p]
Locked in an interrogation room, cut off from the outside world, and questioned by multiple officers, most people would lose their cool.[%p]
They’d start to wonder what effects their words were having, and focus on every movement of the officer who was taking down notes. Even if it wasn’t hot in the room, they’d start to sweat.[%p]
But this girl didn’t respond at all.[%p]
It probably wasn’t because she was used to it.[%p]
If she’d ever been interrogated before, the fingerprints they’d taken would have told them who she was.[%p]
[name]Policewoman[line]“Um, Shinjo-san...”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Hm?”[%p]
[name]Policewoman[line]“Maybe we should take her to...”[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“...”[%p]
To a mental hospital, you mean.[%p]
It made sense. The natural assumption was that the shock of the incident had incapacitated her.[%p]
Shinjo nodded, as if considering it.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Any missing persons reports?”[%p]
This was the second day since the incident.[%p]
If she lived in a normal family environment, someone should have submitted a missing person’s report by now.[%p]
[name]Policewoman[line]“I checked. But no luck.”[%p]
[name]Young Girl[line]“...”[%p]
Shinjo watched the girl as he listened to her talk.[%p]
But the girl didn’t move a muscle.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line](Even mentioning her family didn’t get us anywhere, huh?)[%p]
The normal ways weren’t going to work, then.[%p]
She’d be sent to the hospital by dawn at this rate.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“Thanks. I’ll take care of her. You can go.”[%p]
The other officer couldn’t hide her happiness as she left the room.[%p]
[name]Shinjo[line]“...”[%p]
Shinjo sat down in the chair she’d been sitting in, and took a good look at the girl from the front.[%p]
[name]Young Girl[line]“...”[%p]
She was ignoring him and still looking down at the desk.[%p]
She didn’t even seem to notice or care that the person in front of her had changed.[%p]

After he’d hung up, she’d called back in precisely three minutes, just like she’d promised.[%p]
And then she’d said, “My kid has a message for you.”[%p]

[name]Shinjo[line]“I’ve got a question for you.”[%p]
[name]Young Girl[line]“...”[%p]

[name]Shinjo[line]“Do you have it but didn’t realize it, or have you never had it? Which is it?”[%p]

[name]Shinjo[line]“...!”[%p]

[name]Arimura[line]“Arimura Hinae. I’m a student at Hekiho Academy.”[%p]
