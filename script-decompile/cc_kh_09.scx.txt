[center][margin top="242"]October 17th, 2015. Saturday.[evaluate expr="291EA4B5281400811400"][%e]
It was after 7:00 PM in the evening, but there wasn’t a single train car coming down the Yamanote line.[%p]
And it wasn’t just the trains. The entire city of Shibuya was now a ghost town.[%p]
There was no light, and nothing moved. The town itself was eerily clean.[%p]
That was the big difference between now and six years ago.[%p]
Wakui went inside the pedestrian’s tunnel under the train tracks, listening to the voices of the monsters howling in the distance.[%p]
The night wind blew past him, colder than before.[%p]
The moonlight didn’t reach inside the tunnel, so it was extremely dark.[%p]
Wakui could sense a lone girl in the darkness.[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“Hello. Right on time, I suppose?”[%p]
The girl he’d summoned moved away from the wall she’d been leaning on, then stood with her back to the moonlight.[%p]
Her eyes shone with a strange light that made Wakui shudder.[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“You can have this back. Here.”[%p]
Wakui tossed the baseball he was holding at the girl.[%p]
She didn’t even try to catch it. Instead, it struck her in the chest and fell to the floor, where it bounced several times.[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“Oh, you don’t need it back? Or was it school property?”[%p]
[name]Onoe[line]“Tell me why you attacked Miyashiro Takuru and Kazuki Hana.”[%p]
That was what the girl—Onoe Serika—demanded to know.[%p]
It wasn’t her usual, feminine voice, either. You could hear the arrogance in it.[%p]
Wakui deserved her respect given his position, but she showed no sign of it.[%p]
He didn’t like that at all, but he also didn’t feel like bringing it up.[%p]
The Committee, as well as Wakui, its messenger, had decided to respect Serika’s position. At least, for as long as their interests were aligned.[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“Are you willing to fight the Committee, depending on my answer?”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“You interrupted me back there. That was more than sufficient for the Committee to decide to eliminate you.”[%p]
[name]Onoe[line]“I won’t let you kill Miyashiro Takuru on a whim. If you want to try, you need to tell me why.”[%p]
[name]Onoe[line]“Depending on your reason, I’ll either allow it, or cut ties with you.”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“Allow it, huh? Hahaha... Well, I guess that WAS the deal we made.”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“But you know, he really is all you ever think of, isn’t he? It’s quite impressive.”[%p]
The Committee of 300 was cooperating with Onoe Serika.[%p]
...Or, to be more precise, they were allowing her to get away with what she was doing.[%p]
[name]Onoe[line]“Tell me why.”[%p]
Serika glared directly at Wakui. She didn’t look away for an instant.[%p]
Her face was expressionless, and it was hard to believe that a teenage girl could be so calm in this situation.[%p]
Wakui slumped his shoulders in a slightly exaggerated fashion.[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“Our problem is with Kazuki Hana, not Miyashiro Takuru, you see.”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“She’s gone too far.”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“This is the same nonsense we went through six years ago.”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“We’ve been very quietly performing our experiments here in this city, and now there’s all this chaos.”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“Since she can’t control her power, it’s not fair to put all the blame on her. But still, the fact of the matter is that the delusion she realbooted is what caused this problem.”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“The actions of this one girl have disrupted order in Shibuya, and maybe even the entire world.”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“There are also unconfirmed reports that Nishijou Takumi may be backing her as well.”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“If this matter isn’t settled, not only will the world find out about the Gigalomaniacs, but the whole world might be swallowed up by Kazuki Hana’s delusions.”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“I can see from the look on your face that you think the Committee is overreacting. But that’s just who we are. If the Committee wasn’t this cautious, we never would’ve gotten this far.”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“Thus, we’ve designated Kazuki Hana an enemy of the world.”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“But still, she is a very rare test sample, and it would be a waste to kill her.”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“So the Committee has settled on a plan of driving her insane and capturing her alive.”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“Miyashiro Takuru is just an afterthought. I don’t think there’s any other reason beyond the fact that he was with her.”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“We have our agreement with you as well. I’ve got no intention of killing him. I just wanted to teach him a lesson.”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“Once we have Kazuki Hana, we plan to give him back to you.”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“But of course, if you so wish, we can kill him instead. What do you say?”[%p]
[name]Onoe[line]“...”[%p]
Serika thought for a moment, and lightly kicked her foot against the wall of the tunnel.[%p]
The sound echoed down the tunnel.[%p]
[name]Onoe[line]“You’ve told me your reason. So I’ll allow it.”[%p]
That was her answer.[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“So you’ll let me decide whether to kill him or let him live? Is that it?”[%p]
[name]Onoe[line]“Yes.”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“You’re sure?”[%p]
[name]Onoe[line]“It’s fine.”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“Heh... You really are insane. So as long as Miyashiro Takuru enjoys it, you’re willing to sacrifice the man himself.”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“Or maybe you were simply born with different values than the rest of us?”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“By the way, I’m unfortunately not an entertainer. So I may not be as good at this as you are. Keep that in mind.”[%p]
[name]Onoe[line]“...”[%p]
Serika ignored his joke.[%p]
It was clear that she felt discussions were finished.[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“Can I ask you one question?”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“What do you intend to do after Miyashiro Takuru dies?”[%p]
[name]Onoe[line]“...”[%p]
[name]Onoe[line]“There is nothing for me after that.”[%p]
Serika laughed, then turned around to leave.[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“I see. How noble.”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“Oh, Onoe-kun? There are monsters around, so be careful on your way home.”[%p]
But Serika didn’t even bother looking back.[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“All right. If that’s the deal, maybe I can be a little more open with how I act.”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“If I could lure out Nishijou Takumi, too, that would be ideal.”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“But for now, the problem is Kazuki Hana and Miyashiro Takuru.”[%p]
Wakui took out his phone and dialed a number on the contact list.[%p]
The phone rang for a while before it connected.[%p]
[name]Kurusu[line]“Yes, hello?”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“I’m Mr. Wakui, the student council advisor.”[%p]
[name]Wakui[line]“I need to speak with the student council president for a moment. Is she available?”[%p]
